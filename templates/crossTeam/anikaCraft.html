How to make paper stars

You can make these stars easily as a form of stress relief. You can put them in a jar to use as decoration or strig them up together to create a garland.

Step 1

Get paper strips. You can buy the strips already cut or you can cut them yourself.

Step 2

Fold the paper strip into a knot.

Step 3

Tuck the excess into the flap.

Step 4

Start folding the paper around the pentagon. Follow the natural path of the paper strip.

Step 5

Tuck the excess into the flap.

Step 6

Push the sides of the pentagon inward. This will puff up your star.

Outcome

You can put these stars into a jar or string them up into a garland.
